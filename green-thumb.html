Frost · settings

Read

Frost dates are a climatology drawn from gridded reanalysis, not a guarantee. Your own ground, low pockets, walls, and cold air drainage shift these by days. The planting calendar counts from your average last spring frost. Offsets are typical references and vary by cultivar.
